## SYSTEM NEC PC-98 ##
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - 
ROM files extensions accepted: .d98  .98d  .fdi  .fdd  .2hd  .tfd  .d88  .88d  .hdm  .xdf  .dup  .cmd  .hdi  .thd  .nhd  .hdd  .hdn  .m3u  .zip  .7z  .uce

Put your NEC PC-98 games in this directory that match the above extensions.
Some systems and/or games require BIOS to run properly. BIOS files should be placed in:
   /rcade/share/bios
