## SYSTEM SONY PLAYSTATION ##
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - 
ROM files extensions accepted: .img  .pbp  .cue  .iso  .ccd  .cbn  .m3u  .chd  .zip  .7z  .uce

Put your Sony Playstation games in this directory that match the above extensions.
Some systems and/or games require BIOS to run properly. BIOS files should be placed in:
   /rcade/share/bios
