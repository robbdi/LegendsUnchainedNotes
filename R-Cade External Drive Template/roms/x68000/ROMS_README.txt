## SYSTEM SHARP X68000 ##
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - 
ROM files extensions accepted: .dim  .img  .d88  .88d  .hdm  .dup  .2hd  .xdf  .hdf  .cmd  .m3u  .zip  .7z  .uce

Put your Sharp X68000 games in this directory that match the above extensions.
Some systems and/or games require BIOS to run properly. BIOS files should be placed in:
   /rcade/share/bios
